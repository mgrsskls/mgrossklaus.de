---

```css
font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Segoe UI", Roboto, Helvetica,
  Arial, sans-serif;
```

or

```css
font: 1rem/1.2 BlinkMacSystemFont, -apple-system, "Segoe UI", Roboto, Helvetica,
  Arial, sans-serif;
```

The shorthand version cannot start with `-apple-system`!
